About Bella Vista

Our Story

The history of restaurants traces back to ancient Roman thermopolia and 11th-century China, but the modern concept emerged in 18th-century Paris with A. Boulanger's 1765 shop selling restorative broths, leading to the term "restaurant".

The French Revolution spurred chefs to open public eateries, establishing menus, separate tables, and individualized service, a model that spread globally, evolving through innovations like American diners, fast-food chains, and modern diverse dining experiences.
